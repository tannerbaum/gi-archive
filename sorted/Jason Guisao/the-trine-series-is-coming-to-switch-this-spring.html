The Trine Series Is Coming To Switch This Spring

by Jason Guisao on Feb 13, 2019 at 01:15 PM

Share

FacebookPost
TwitterTweet
EmailEmail
CommentComment

Frozenbyte plans to release the entirety of its Trine Series on the Nintendo Switch this Spring. Already available for physical pre-orders, Trine: Enchanted Edition, Trine 2: Complete Story, and Trine 3: The Artifacts of Power explore the whimsical adventures of franchise protagonists Amadeus, Pontius, and Zoya. Showcasing Frozenbytes knack for puzzle-platformers, the bundle is priced at $39.99. 

Trine 2: Complete Story, however, will be released early through the eShop for Switch owners on February 18. As a stand-alone title, Trine 2 is $16.99. With over 20 levels of hand-drawn content and a local as well as online co-op mode, players will finally be able to take their favorite fantastical side-scrollers on the go. 

 

If these three games cant satiate your Trine hunger, dont worry. Last fall, Frozenbyte confirmed the development of Trine 4 which will also be available on Switch (alongside PC, PlayStation4 and Xbox One) later this year.
