
  Square Enix Confirms Octopath Traveler PC Release Date 






            Octopath Traveler released last summer on Switch. Recently, details about an upcoming PC port leaked on the Korean Ratings Board. Additionally, Square Enix posted a blog revealing the game's PC release date, but the post was quickly taken down.

Today, however, Square dropped a trailer that legitimizes the aforementioned PC rumors: Octopath Traveler launches for PC on June 7. For those of you excited about the announcement, check out this in-depth analysis on how the game's music plays on nostalgia.
