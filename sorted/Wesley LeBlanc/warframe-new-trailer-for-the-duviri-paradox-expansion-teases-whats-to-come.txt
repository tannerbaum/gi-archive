
  Warframe: New Trailer For The Duviri Paradox Expansion Teases What’s To Come






            Digital Extremes has released a new trailer for its upcoming Warframe expansion, The Duviri Paradox. 

This trailer aired during today’s Summer Game Fest showcase, and it offers a mysterious look at the highly-anticipated Warframe expansion, teasing more to come during TennoCon 2022. More specifically, if this trailer has you excited about The Duviri Paradox, you’ll be able to check out the first look at gameplay for it at 5 p.m. ET on July 16.
