
  Travis Strikes Again: No More Heroes Coming To PS4 And PC This October






Suda51's Travis Strikes Again: No More Heroes, which launched on Switch this past January, is soon coming to other platforms this fall.

The newest entry to the eclectic series is releasing on PS4 and PC on October 17. It takes place seven years after the events of No More Heroes 2: Desperate Struggle.
