
  Xbox One Controllers With Fresh Looks Coming This Fall






Two new stylish Xbox One controllers are on their way. One is with a Sports Blue color scheme and the other is Night Ops Camo, both pictured above. 

They each cost $69.99. Sports Blue is available as of September 17 and Night Ops Camo is available on October 8.

The Night Ops Camo controller has a camouflage design with a gold D-pad, whereas Sports Blue has a modern and geometric style.
