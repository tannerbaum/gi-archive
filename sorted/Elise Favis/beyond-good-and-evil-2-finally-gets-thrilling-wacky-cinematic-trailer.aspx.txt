
  Beyond Good & Evil 2 Finally Gets Thrilling, Wacky Cinematic Trailer






Beyond Good and Evil fans have been waiting for a sequel for a long time, and Ubisoft surprised the crowd today at its press conference with a surprise cinematic trailer for Beyond Good & Evil 2.
This prequel takes place before Jade, the protagonist of the original game, is born. The trailer starts off in a shady restaurant and then evolves into an action-packed chase where two characters, a monkey and a woman, evade missiles on a flying motorcycle in a futuristic landscape.
