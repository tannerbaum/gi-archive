
  Co-Op Puzzle Game 39 Days To Mars Lands On PS4 This Winter






Co-op puzzle game 39 Days to Mars is heading to PS4 soon. Developer It's Anecdotal didn't give a specific release date, but it's planned to arrive at some point this winter.

39 Days to Mars puts you in the shoes of two 19th century explorers who pilot an unreliable spaceship to Mars. However, things don't go as planned. The steam engine runs out of coal, the ship's cat creates mishaps, and other major problems arise. You (with a friend or own your own with A.I.) solve each issue to help make the journey smoother.

39 Days to Mars is also available on Switch and PC. You can read our review here.
