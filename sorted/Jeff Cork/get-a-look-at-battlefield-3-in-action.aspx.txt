
  Get A Look At Battlefield 3 In Action






We brought you a look at Battlefield 3 in our March issue of Game Informer, but now you can see the game in action for yourself. EA and DICE have released a short video that includes a few fleeting glimpses of gameplay from the upcoming shooter. While you won't see any of the game's destructible environments in the clip, you will get a taste of combat.
