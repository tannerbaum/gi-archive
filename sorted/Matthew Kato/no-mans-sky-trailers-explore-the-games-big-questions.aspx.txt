If you've been wondering what there is to do in a procedurally generated universe of more than 18 quintillion planets you can explore, then this new trailer series for No Man's Sky should help you out.

The series' debut trailer is all about the ability to explore the universe, scan animals, and wander planets filled with mysteries waiting to be uncovered. Future videos will cover trading, combat, and survival.

No Man's Sky comes out on August 9 for PlayStation 4 and PC.
