
  First Xbox Series X Game Shown – Senua's Saga: Hellblade II






            Today Phil Spencer announced the upcoming Xbox system – Xbox Series X, and he also showed the first public debut of a Series X game – Senua's Saga: Hellblade II.

The game's footage at the Game Awards was created in-engine, and it showed beautiful, cinematic scenes of creepy rituals to a terrifying demon by an isolated cult.

Spencer said the game was early in development, but it's unknown if it'll be a launch title for the holiday 2020 system or will come after.
