Moto Racer 4


  First Teaser Trailer And PlayStation VR Compatibility Announced




Racing game Moto Racer 4 is in development for compatibility with PlayStation VR, and along with the news come the game's first teaser trailer showing what its like to be put in the driver's seat. 
Players will be able to take to the tracks on both high-speed roads and dirt motocross courses in virtual reality. The game features 15 single and multiplayer game modes including time trails and championship, and will also feature split-screen multiplayer mode for offline racing.
