
  Vampire Survivors Co-op On Switch | New Gameplay Today






            Vampire Survivors is finally coming to the platform it was always destined for: the Nintendo Switch. Alongside the full game and its DLC being fully playable on Nintendo's platform, it also includes a new co-op mode. Join Game Informer's Kyle Hilliard and Marcus Stewart to see if the Switch can handle the game firing on all cylinders and to see some of the co-op in action. Vampire Survivors releases on Switch August 17.
