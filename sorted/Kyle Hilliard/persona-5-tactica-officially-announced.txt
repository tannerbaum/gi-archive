
  Persona 5 Tactica Officially Announced






            After leaking earlier this week alongside the remake of Persona 3, the Persona 5 tactics game is now official.

The Persona 5 tactics game, called Persona 5 Tactica, releases this year, on November 17. It appears to be a continuation of the stories from Persona 5 and Persona 5 Strikers and adds a new mysterious member to the crew, but with a new chibi art style and grid-based tactics gameplay. Persona 5 Tactica will also be available on Xbox Game Pass.
