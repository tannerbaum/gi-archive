  Retro City Rampage Arrives On 3DS In February




Recent tweets from the official Retro City Rampage twitter account 
reveals that the game should be coming to 3DS next month.

@NintendoTweet February, but no specific date yet known
— Retro City Rampage (@RetroCR) January 24, 2014
The lone developer behind the game, Brian Provinciano, says he isn't sure of the game's exact release date, but when it does release it will be $9.99.
