
  Disney Speedstorm | New Gameplay Today






            Disney Speedstorm is an upcoming, free-to-play kart racer that takes advantage of Disney's impressive bench of characters and intellectual properties to create a kart racing game. Join Charles Harte and myself as we take a look at a few races, discuss the pitfalls of free-to-play, all while marveling at the visuals and impressive gameplay.

Disney Speedstorm releases on April 18 on just about every platform.
