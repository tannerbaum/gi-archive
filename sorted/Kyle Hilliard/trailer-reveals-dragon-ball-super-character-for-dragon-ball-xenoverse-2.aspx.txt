Dragon Ball Super began airing in Japan last year and has not yet been localized for North America. But that's not stopping Dragon Ball Xenoverse 2 from featuring one of the show's characters.
I don't know much about Goku Black as I would like to learn his origin by watching the show, but I know he's a bad guy, and I know he's giving Super Saiyan God Vegeta a hard time in the short combat clip below.
