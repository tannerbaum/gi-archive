
  Donkey Kong Country Fans Should Take Note Of Nikoderiko: The Magical World | New Gameplay Today






            Nikoderiko: The Magical World is a classic mascot platformer heavily inspired by games like Donkey Kong Country and Crash Bandicoot. As Niko you swap between 2D and 3D sections of levels, collect four golden letters, find hidden barrels that send you to play minigames and more. The game also features music by Donkey Kong Country composer, David Wise.
