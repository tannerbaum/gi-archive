
  The First Three Tomb Raider Games Are Getting Remastered For Switch






            Tomb Raider I, II, and III are coming to Switch with visual overhauls, but will otherwise retain the same gameplay. In fact, you will be able to switch back and forth between the old and new visual styles.

Tomb Raider I, II, and III Remastered will be available on Switch (as well as PlayStation, Xbox, and PC) on February 14.
