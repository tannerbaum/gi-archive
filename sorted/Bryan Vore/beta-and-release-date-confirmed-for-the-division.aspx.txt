
  Beta And Release Date Confirmed For The Division






The wait for The Division is narrowing with both a beta and an official release date.
The official release of the game will be March 8, 2016. Ubisoft didn't say when the beta would be coming, but it will arrive before the game is released on PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC.
Stay tuned for more details on the new Dark Zone mode very soon.
