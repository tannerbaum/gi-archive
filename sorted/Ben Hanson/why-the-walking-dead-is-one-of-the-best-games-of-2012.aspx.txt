
  Why The Walking Dead Is One Of The Best Games Of 2012






Throughout the month we will be posting articles and videos dedicated to celebrating the greatest games of the year. When constructing our list of the top 50 games for the latest issue of the magazine, The Walking Dead from Telltale Games was a shoe-in. Carried on the back of the game's excellent writing, this episodic adventure grabbed the attention of gamers across the globe and garnered Telltale Games their first runaway hit.
Game Informer's Andrew Reiner, Kim Wallace, Ben Reeves, and Tim Turi put The Walking Dead near the top of their list of favorite games this year, watch the video below to hear them discuss the impact it had on them.
