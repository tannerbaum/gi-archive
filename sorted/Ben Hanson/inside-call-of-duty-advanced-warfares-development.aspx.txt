
  Inside Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare's Development






With our June cover story on Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare, we're rolling out a month of content highlighting different aspects of the game. Since this is a new primary developer for the Call of Duty series, we want explain who the folks at Sledgehammer Games are and where they come from. We spoke with studio heads Glen Schofield and Michael Condrey and senior development director Aaron Halon about the team's history and their approach to tackling the development of the one of the biggest games of 2014.
Watch the video below to see inside Sledgehammer Games and learn how their work on Modern Warfare 3 helped inform their take on a brand new universe within Call of Duty.
