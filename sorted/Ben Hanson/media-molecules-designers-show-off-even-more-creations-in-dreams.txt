
  Media Molecule's Designers Show Off Even More Creations In Dreams






            Game Informer's latest cover story on Dreams has talked a lot about the power of the creative tools being built by Media Molecule and shown plenty of gameplay from the upcoming PS4 game. With the tools packed into Dreams, however, there's never a shortage of new gameplay to show. In the video above, Dreams' creative director Mark Healey is joined by senior principal designer John Beech, designer Christophe Villedieu, and web developer Tom Dallaway to show a selection of games that they've been building on the side.
