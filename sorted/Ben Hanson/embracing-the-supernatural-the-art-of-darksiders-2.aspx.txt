
  Embracing The Supernatural: The Art Of Darksiders II






While you may have already seen Joe Madureira demonstrate and explain his own drawing process, there is an entire team of unsung heroes hard at work in the art department of Darksiders II. Led by art director Han Randhawa, the team at Vigil Games is exploring the possibilities of the afterlife as they leave Earth behind and dream up the most interesting locations imaginable. Watch the video to hear about the artistic direction for the sequel, a definition of the "Darksiders style", and to learn the process of how the land of the dead is brought to life.
