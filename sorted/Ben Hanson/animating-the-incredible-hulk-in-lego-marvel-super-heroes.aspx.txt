
  Animating The Incredible Hulk In Lego Marvel Super Heroes






Lego designed their version of the the Incredible Hulk when creating the sets for their Lego Marvel Super Heroes toy line, but now the development team at Traveller's Tales has to animate him. As the first of Lego's "big figs," the Hulk was a satisfying challenge for Paul Munro and the the rest of the animation team.
Watch the video feature below to learn how the team studied and animated the characters of Iron Man and the Hulk, and see a glimpse at early gameplay footage of the next Lego game.
