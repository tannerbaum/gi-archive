
  48 Rapid-Fire Questions With Super Mario Maker 2's Takashi Tezuka






            You should know the name Takashi Tezuka. He directed a handful of the greatest games of all time, such as Super Mario Bros. 3, The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past, Super Mario World, The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening, and more! While at E3 2019, we got an opportunity to speak with Tezuka about Super Mario Maker 2, Yoshi's anatomy, and his relationship with Shigeru Miyamoto. We hope you enjoy the interview!
