
  GI Show – Spider-Man, Ernest Cline, Adam Boyes Interview






Welcome back to The Game Informer Show! On this episode, Ben Hanson, Leo Vader, Kyle Hilliard, and Andrew Reiner share new, exclusive details on Insomniac's Spider-Man based on their gameplay-filled cover story trip. Then we talk about the latest round of the ongoing rivalry between PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds and Epic's Fortnite, including PUBG's new "Savage" map. Elise joins the podcast to talk about Minit and also answer a bunch of fun community emails, and we're also joined by Ready Player One's author Ernest Cline to talk about Spielberg and games, then Iron Galaxy's Adam Boyes to discuss Extinction, working at Capcom and Sony, and his career as a whole. It's a big show – tell a friend if you enjoy it!
