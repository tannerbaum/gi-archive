
  Darksiders II: Lead Designer Haydn Dalton Interview






From England to Texas, Commodore 64 to PS3, Haydn Dalton has had a full experience within the gaming industry. As lead designer on Darksiders II, his past experiences are invaluable for informing his everyday choices that funnel into the creation of Darksiders II. Game Informer's own Phil Kollar spoke with Haydn in the lobby of Vigil Games in a wide-ranging interview that covers everything from Ocarina of Time to 50 Cent.
