
  Overseeing The Production Of Assassin's Creed III






Imagine trying to oversee the entire production of Assassin's Creed III. That includes not only the developer-filled fortress at Ubisoft Montreal, but four other studios spread across the globe as well. The breadth of the Assassin's Creed series has outgrown the production capabilities of a single studio, and senior producer François Pelland is the man responsible for keeping all of the other studios on track. Game Informer's Matt Miller sat down with François to better understand the production process that will deliver Assassin's Creed III to the public this fall.
