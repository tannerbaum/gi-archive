
  Dungeons & Dragons Gets Magic: The Gathering Sourcebook






            Wizards of the Coast announced Guildmasters’ Guide to Ravnica, a new Dungeons & Dragons sourcebook that brings the classic pen-and-paper RPG to Magic: The Gathering's universe. The book provides players with the lore and rules they'll need to play in Magic: The Gathering's Ravinica, a worldwide city where it's always raining. 

Guildmasters’ Guide to Ravnica marks the first time the series have officially crossed over. The book is set to release on November 20 for $49.95 alongside a set of Guildmasters’ Guide to Ravnica dice and a "maps and miscellany" resource.

Wizards of the Coast also announced Wayfinder’s Guide to Eberron, a PDF sourcebook available now that updates the Eberron campaign setting for fifth edition D&D.
