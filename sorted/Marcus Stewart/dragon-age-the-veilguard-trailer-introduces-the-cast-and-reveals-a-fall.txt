
  Dragon Age: The Veilguard Trailer Introduces The Cast And Reveals A Fall Release Window






            Dragon Age: The Veilguard (formerly known as Dreadwolf) received its first cinematic trailer, introducing its cast during today's Xbox Games Showcase. 

The video serves as an action-packed roll call for the companions accompanying the protagonist. The colorful cast includes Harding the Scout, Neve the Detective, Lucanis the Mage Killer, Bellara the Veil Jumper, Emmrich the Necromancer, Davrin the Warden, and Taash the Dragon Hunter. Watch them get in fights, from bar brawls to taking on a dragon, in the trailer below.
