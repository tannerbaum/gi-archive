
  Horizon Forbidden West, The Quarry, More Join PlayStation Plus Extra And Premium Catalog






            Those subscribed to PlayStation Plus’ Extra and Premium tiers have an impressive batch of new monthly games coming next week. That includes some of 2022’s strongest titles, such as Horizon Forbidden West and Supermassive Games’ summer camp slasher, The Quarry. Old-school RPG fans will be especially pleased to see the new PlayStation 1 classics joining the library. 

Each title listed below becomes available on February 21. We've included links to our reviews for applicable titles for those seeking an idea of what to expect from each game.
