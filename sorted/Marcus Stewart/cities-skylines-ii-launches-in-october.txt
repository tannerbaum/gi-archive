
  Cities: Skylines II Launches In October






            Cities: Skylines II has a release date, and it’s coming on October 24.

A new trailer at today’s Xbox Games Showcase showed off some of the city-building gameplay, which, according to developer Colossal’s previous descriptions, features “a wealth of construction and customization options and advanced modding capabilities.” You can be the judge of that yourself in the video below.
