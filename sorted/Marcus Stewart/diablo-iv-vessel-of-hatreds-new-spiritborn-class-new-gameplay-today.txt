
  Diablo IV: Vessel Of Hatred's New Spiritborn Class | New Gameplay Today






            In this episode of New Gameplay Today, editor Marcus Stewart shares his time playing Diablo IV: Vessel of Hatred's new Spiritborn class with Kyle Hilliard. The martial arts-focused class has the ability to tap into the Spirit Realm and harness the power of four powerful beasts – jaguar, gorilla, eagle, and centipede – to battle the forces of Hell. Watch us take the class for a spin and discuss why it might be our favorite in the game. Be sure to also read our in-depth preview of the Spirtborn class here.
