
  New Trailer Breaks Down Breaking Apart Enemies In The Surge 2






            The Surge, a sci-fi Souls-like from Deck13 Interactive, held a promising premise and foundation but failed to execute on many of its best ideas. The end result was an entertaining if middling hack and slash adventure brought down by grinding repetition. However, the sequel is looking to expand on what the original did well and fix a lot of what it didn't.
