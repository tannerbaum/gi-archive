
  Remastered Bulletstorm Revealed As Bulletstorm: Full Clip Edition






The rumored (and possibly leaked) remastered of Bulletstorm has finally been unveiled at The Game Awards as Bulletstorm: Full Clip Edition. Both People Can Fly, the game's developer, and Gearbox are working on the game and it will be available on April 7 for PS4, Xbox One, and PC.
