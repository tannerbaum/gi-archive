
  Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney Trilogy Out Now, Watch The Launch Trailer






Capcom's Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney Trilogy is out now on the Nintendo eShop for Nintendo 3DS, and Capcom released a new trailer to commemorate the occasion. The game, which includes Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney, Justice For All, and Trials and Tribulations, is available as a download for $29.99. As of right now no physical release is planned.
The trailer shows clips from all three DS titles, remastered with higher resolution graphics, introducing Phoenix as well as rival prosecutors including Miles Edgeworth.
