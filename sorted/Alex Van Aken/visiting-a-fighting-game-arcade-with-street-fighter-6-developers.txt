
  Visiting A Fighting Game Arcade With Street Fighter 6 Developers






            In this exclusive interview from our recent trip to Japan, Game Informer and Street Fighter 6 developers Takayuki Nakayama and Shuhei Matsumoto visit Taito Station Nippombashi, a multi-level arcade in a bustling Osaka shopping district. The arcade holds significance for Nakayama, with ties to his early years on Capcom's fighting team.
