Yomawari: Night Alone


  Monsters And Wanderers Appear In The Night 




Yomawari: Night Alone
 is a unique horror experience from the makers of Disgaea that puts players in control of a young girl looking for her family in a town 
haunted by ancient Japanese spirits. 
The latest teaser trailer for the Nippon Ichi game details the various sorts of monsters 
that appear throughout players' exploration of the town. Some creatures will try to scare and follow you, while others are lost 
spirits with tragic pasts that you will need to solve. Check them all 
out for yourself in the footage below.
