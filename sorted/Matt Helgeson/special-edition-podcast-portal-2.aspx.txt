
  Special Edition Podcast: Portal 2






As part of our month-long online blowout of Portal 2, Valve's sequel to its groundbreaking action/puzzle hit, we proudly present our Special Edition: Portal 2 Podcast. For this podcast, we decided to let you, the readers of Game Informer and GI.com, put our editors on the hot seat. Host Matt Helgeson reads your questions to Portal 2 cover story author Meagan VanBurkleo, Ben Reeves, and Dan Ryckert. Listen in to get some of their insights, speculation, and inside scoops on this upcoming blockbuster.
