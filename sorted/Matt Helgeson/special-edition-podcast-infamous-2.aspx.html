Special Edition Podcast: Infamous 2

by Matt Helgeson on Jun 28, 2010 at 12:13 PM

Share

FacebookPost
TwitterTweet
EmailEmail
CommentComment

Infamous 2 is on fire! Our cover story kicked off the hype, but it only got bigger following the game's strong showing at E3. We've been doing bonus content about many of the game's aspects all month, and now you can hear cover story author Joe Juba, Ben Reeves, and Dan Ryckert answer reader questions about the game in this special edition Game Informer Show. You don't want to miss this, so start listening!
