
  Special Edition Podcast: Army Of Two: The Devil's Cartel






Visceral Montreal executive producer Julian Beak and producer Zach Mumbach answer your burning Army of Two: The Devil's Cartel questions in this special-edition podcast.

The show is hosted by Game Informer's Jeff Cork (who wrote the cover story) and Ben Hanson. It covers everything from the game's approach to competitive multiplayer, the decisions behind shifting away from Salem and Rios, and the game's tone. And, of course, panda sex.
