
  Special Edition Podcast: Assassin's Creed: Revelations






Our cover story on Assassin's Creed: Revelations has created a stir among the millions of fans of Ubisoft's cryptic action/adventure series. As always, AC:R blends science fiction with voluminous historical research to further the adventures of Ezio. This game promises to end Ezio's saga, so many have questions as to what this new title brings to the table. We solicited questions from readers on our site, and compiled the best of them for this special edition podcast. Host Matt Helgeson reads your questions cover story to writer Matt Miller and GI editor Joe Juba, both of whom have seen the game in action, on some of the most important aspects of the game, from the plot to the multiplayer.

For more on Assassin's Creed: Revelations, check out our hub page for a host of special bonus content.
