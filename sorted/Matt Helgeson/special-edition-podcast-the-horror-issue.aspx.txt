
  Special Edition Podcast: The Horror Issue






In this week's podcast, we turn the discussion to our recent Horror Issue, and talk about the scariest games of all time.

Host Matt Helgeson is joined by Jeff Cork, who wrote about Alien Isolation in the new issue, as well as GI's expert in all things horror, Tim Turi. We cover everything from Splatterhouse to the upcoming The Evil Within, so buckle up.
