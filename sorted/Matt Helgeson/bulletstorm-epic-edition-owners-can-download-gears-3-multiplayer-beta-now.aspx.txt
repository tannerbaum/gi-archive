
  Bulletstorm: Epic Edition Owners Can Download Gears 3 Multiplayer Beta Now






If you were one of the many that purchased the Epic Edition of Bulletstorm, today is the day. The Epic Edition came free with a Gears of War 3 multiplayer beta invite, which can be redeemed in Bulletstorm starting now.Just pop Bulletstorm into your 360, and click the "Gears of War MP Beta" link on the main menu. This will prompt a download of the beta. In addition, starting next week, players that preordered Gears of War at GameStop will be able to access the beta next week.[GameStop is the parent company of Game Informer]
