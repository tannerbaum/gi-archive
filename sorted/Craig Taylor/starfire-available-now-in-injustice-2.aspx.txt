
  Starfire Is Available Now In Injustice 2






Injustice 2's latest fighter, Starfire, is now playable for owners of the Injustice 2 Ultimate Edition, the Digital Deluxe Edition, the Ultimate Pack, or Fighter Pack #1. She will also be available for individual purchase on August 14.
For a look at how she plays, check out the gameplay trailer below. She has flight, plenty of projectiles, and a Nova-esque human rocket charge.
