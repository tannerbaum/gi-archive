  Bastion Developer Supergiant Announces Next Game, Pyre




Platform
PlayStation 4, PC

Publisher
Supergiant Games

Developer
Supergiant Games

Release
July 25, 2017

Rating
Everyone 10+

Supergiant Games has announced its third game. Pyre follows Bastion and Transistor, and it’ll be out in 2017.
Pyre takes place in a “mystical purgatory,” in which your band of exiles is attempting to free itself. You’ll compete in the Rites, and attempt to learn why they exist.
