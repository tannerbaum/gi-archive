Dead Rising Triple Pack

Dead Rising Re-Releases Confirmed For September Launch
by Mike Futter on Aug 09, 2016 at 06:24 AM

Share

FacebookPost
TwitterTweet
EmailEmail
CommentComment
Platform PlayStation 4, Xbox One
Publisher Capcom
Developer Capcom
Release September 13, 2017
Rating Rating Pending

The “placeholder” date for a trio of Dead Rising re-releases has now been confirmed by Capcom. The zombie-slaying romps will be available in September.

PlayStation 4 and Xbox One owners will get access to Dead Rising, Dead Rising 2, and Dead Rising 2: Off The Record. The latter two have already been released on PC, though the first game in the franchise will make its debut alongside the console launches.

Sorry, this video is not available

You can look for all of these on September 13. And if that’s not enough for you, Dead Rising 4 will be out on Xbox One and PC on December 6.

Each game is priced at $20. On console, the three games are also available in a bundle for $50. On PC, you can right now purchase a Collector’s Pack for $90 that includes Dead Rising 2, Dead Rising 2: Off the Record, Dead Rising 3 and all its DLC, as well as additional DLC for Dead Rising 2 and Off the Record. Physical releases of Dead Rising and Dead Rising 2 are coming for console on September 13 and 27 respectively.
