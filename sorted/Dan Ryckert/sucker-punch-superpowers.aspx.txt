
  Sucker Punch's Favorite (And Lamest) Superpowers






It probably comes at no surprise that Sucker Punch is filled with comic fans. After all, they created one of the best superhero games of all time with the original Infamous. While Cole's powers may have been constricted to electricity-based attacks and maneuvers, the team is obviously familiar with the collection of abilities seen in the comic medium's past.
We spoke with the team and asked them what their favorite superpower is, as well as their pick for the lamest. Check it out below.
