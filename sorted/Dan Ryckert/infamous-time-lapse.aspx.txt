
  A Time Lapse Look At The New Cole






If you've seen our latest cover, you've probably noticed that 
the city isn't the only thing that's been changed in Infamous 2. Our 
favorite electric superhero Cole MacGrath has moved down south and 
adopted a brand new look (and ditched the jacket to go along with the 
warmer climate). One of the team members involved in the change, artist Mike Haney, was kind enough to let us roll camera as he put together a rough 
conceptual freehand sketch showcasing the new Cole. Start the video 
below for our time-lapse look of the process.
