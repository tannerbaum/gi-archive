Radiant Historia: Perfect Chronology


  Return To The Past In Radiant Historia: Perfect Chronology 




Radiant Historia launched on the DS way back in 2011, but now in early 2018 players have a chance to go back and experience the adventure again in a whole new way. 
While Radiant Historia: Perfect Chronology is a port of the original, it features additional story and gameplay content, updated presentation, and a smattering of many other tweaks and bonuses.
