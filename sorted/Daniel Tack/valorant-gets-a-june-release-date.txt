
  Valorant Gets A June Release Date






            A summer launch date was anticipated for Riot Games' upcoming free-to-play tactical shooter Valorant, and now we've got the details on that day - June 2.

Players in the beta can enjoy the game until May 28, where it will close down to get everything in place for launch day. Currently, Valorant is only available on PC.

Valorant is expected to add new characters, new maps, and new modes(!) shortly after launch.
