Shin Megami Tensei: Strange Journey Redux


  Tackle Demons And Dungeons In Shin Megami Tensei: Strange Journey Redux




The sci-fi RPG in the Shin Megami Tensei universe Strange Journey landed back in 2010, and now (like many other Atlus titles it seems!) is ready for a revamped port to the 3DS. 
What can we expect? New story content, a new dungeon (nice!), and additional endings! Are you prepared for a... stranger journey? We'll find out in early 2018.
Until then, you can check out the trailer below!
