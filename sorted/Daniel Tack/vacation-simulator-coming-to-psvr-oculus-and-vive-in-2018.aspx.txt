
  Vacation Simulator Coming To PlayStation VR, Oculus, And Vive In 2018






At the Game Awards 2017, Owlchemy Labs announced a new VR title, Vacation Simulator. With a quick trailer that featured some beach antics and style, the follow-up to the much-lauded VR title Job Simulator is poised for a warm welcome. On sunny beaches! With like, fishing, and beach balls, and water.
Vacation Simulator is coming to PlayStation VR, Oculus, and Vive.

Check out more at the official Owlchemy Labs site!
