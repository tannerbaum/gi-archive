Paper Mario: Color Splash


  New Trailer Sets The Stage For Prism Island




If you've been hearing about Paper Mario: Color Splash, but still aren't completely understanding the story or the gameplay, Nintendo has you covered. The publisher released a new video showcasing the main narrative points to the upcoming Paper Mario: Color Splash, as well as a rundown of the kinds of things players should expect to encounter during gameplay.
From giant Chain Chomps to colorless Toads, this introduction trailer entitled "The Adventure Unfolds" gives you a handy rundown of the essentials of the newest Paper Mario game. You can see the new video below. For our latest hands-on impressions, check out our preview from PAX West. Paper Mario: Color Splash hits Wii U on October 7.
