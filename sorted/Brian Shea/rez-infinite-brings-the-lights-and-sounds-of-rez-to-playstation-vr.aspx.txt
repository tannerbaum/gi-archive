
  Rez Infinite Brings The Lights And Sounds Of Rez To PlayStation VR






The sensory overload that is Rez is taking it to the next level by surrounding you with the lights and sounds though the new technology of virtual reality. Rez Infinite brings the classic game back in a way that is more immersive and overwhelming than ever in PlayStation VR. 
While no release date has been announced yet, PlayStation VR is set to launch sometime in 2016.
