
  New Octopath Traveler Demo Coming Soon For Switch






            Nintendo announced during their E3 presentation today that a new Octopath Traveler demo, Square-Enix's JRPG hearkening back to old school games in the genre, will be released on June 14. 

The demo's save data can be transferred to the full game when it releases in July.
