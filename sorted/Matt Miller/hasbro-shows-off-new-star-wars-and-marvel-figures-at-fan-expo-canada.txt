
  Hasbro Shows Off New Star Wars And Marvel Figures At Fan Expo Canada






            Hasbro today revealed images of several new figures making their way to the popular 6-inch Star Wars Black Series and Marvel Legends lines.

Fans have had a lot of good things to say about both lines, thanks to excellent sculpts and coloring, as well as the broad array of characters the lines have explored.

Check out the gallery below of the following figures:

Star Wars

Star Wars The Black Series Dryden Vos 6-Inch Figure (Available early 2019)  
Star Wars The Vintage Collection Phasma Figure (Available early 2019)  

Marvel

Marvel Legends Weapon X Figure (Available 2019)
Marvel Legends Guardian Figure (Available 2019)
Marvel Legends Wendigo Build-A-Figure (Available 2019)
Marvel Legends Mystique Figure (Available 2019 – exclusively at Walgreens in the US
