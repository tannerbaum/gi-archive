
  Anatomy Of A Character: Kratos






Over the course of the God of War series, Sony has established Kratos as one of the great video game protagonists. His unquenchable rage and quest for vengeance has established him as the ultimate expression of unstoppable power and brutality. For the trilogy-ending installment, we take a look at Kratos from head to toe, to see what makes him such a great video game character. (Be warned: God of War I & II spoilers ahead)
