Wesley LeBlanc

Associate Editor

Wesley's journey into video games started on the SNES with Super Mario World and NBA Jam. He graduated into playing whatever RPGs his older brother finished (looking at you, Final Fantasy 7) before getting his hands on a PS2. That's when he discovered that video games were his favorite thing ever. Today, he especially enjoys RPGs, immersive sims, action-adventure games, and anything even remotely related to pirates. Not content to simply playing games, Wesley obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Multimedia Journalism from the University of North Florida in 2017 with the intent to find a home for his games writing. That home is Game Informer. Here, you'll find his thoughts on the latest games, news coverage, and so much more. Oh, and he will never give up hope for a PS5 patch for Bloodborne (please, From Software?).
