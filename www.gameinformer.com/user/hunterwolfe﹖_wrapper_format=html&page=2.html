Hunter Wolfe

Hunter cut his teeth on early platformers like Crash Bandicoot and Tomb Raider (admittedly, he had to use the “level-skip” cheat code a couple hundred times). These early titles cultivated his love for video games, which later blossomed into things like cosplay, fanfiction, and now games press. Hunter studied journalism at Shippensburg University and wrote news and features for GamingTrend.com for two years. When he’s not working, Hunter is voraciously reading nonfiction. Or he’s probably on his Xbox. He still plays Tomb Raider games, but he doesn’t need the level-skip cheat anymore.
