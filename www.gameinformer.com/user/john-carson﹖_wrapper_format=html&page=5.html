John Carson

Associate Editor

Born in West Allis, WI and transplanted to Minnesota in his early years, John has always seen games as a way to bond with people. Some long-time fans of Game Informer might know him from the various Guinness World Record attempts or early GI livestreams from years ago. He's written on and off for video game websites in the past, and recently has spent time working as an A/V service technician. Games were always on his mind and led him to pursuing a position at Game Informer. Some of his favorite games he played in the last year are Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 1+2, Ghost of Tsushima, Monster Hunter Rise, Trails in the Sky, and Loop Hero. John can never rest until he finishes Chakan: The Forever Man for Sega Genesis.
