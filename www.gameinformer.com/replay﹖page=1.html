Replay is Game Informers weekly journey into the rich history of video games. GI editors dust off games from every genre and platform imaginable in an attempt to recapture the magic of the games weve all grown up with. Join us every Friday at 2pm CST to relive fond (and not so fond) memories from gamings glorious past.
