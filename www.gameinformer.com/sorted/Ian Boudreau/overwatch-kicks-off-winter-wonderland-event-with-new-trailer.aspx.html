Overwatch Kicks Off Winter Wonderland Event With New Trailer

by Ian Boudreau on Dec 13, 2016 at 07:59 AM

Share

FacebookPost
TwitterTweet
EmailEmail
CommentComment

The Overwatch gang is getting into the holiday spirit in a new trailer kicking off the Winter Wonderland event.

Winter Wonderland is now live on all platforms and runs through January 2. As with Overwatch's prior special events for the Summer Games and Halloween, Winter Wonderland includes new hero skins and items, redecorated Hanamura and King's Row maps, and a special 6v6 brawl mode starring Mei in a massive snowball fight. You can read all the details on the Overwatch blog.

The Winter Wonderland update also includes major changes to Symmetra's balancing and abilities.
