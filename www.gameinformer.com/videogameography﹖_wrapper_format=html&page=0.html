Each season of Video Gameography tackles a popular game series and provides a game-by-game breakdown of its development history and lore. Every Saturday at 9 AM CST, join the host, Marcus Stewart, alongside special industry guests as they provide educational and entertaining deep dives into your favorite franchises.
