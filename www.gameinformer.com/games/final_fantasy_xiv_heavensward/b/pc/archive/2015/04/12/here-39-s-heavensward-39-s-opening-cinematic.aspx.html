Final Fantasy XIV: Heavensward

Here's Heavensward's Opening Cinematic
by Kyle Hilliard on Apr 12, 2015 at 10:51 AM
Publisher: Square Enix
Developer: Square Enix
Release: June 23, 2015
Rating: Rating Pending
Platform: PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, PlayStation 2, PC

Share

FacebookPost
TwitterTweet
EmailEmail
CommentComment

Square Enix has released the opening cinematic for Final Fantasy XIV's upcoming expansion, Heavensward.

You can check out the cinematic below which, fair warning, contains some scenes from the ending of Final Fantasy XIV: A Realm Reborn's main story, so look out for spoilers if you're trying to avoid them. Unsurprisingly, it looks great, living up to Square Enix's long-ago established standard of quality for pre-rendered cutscenes.

Final Fantasy XIV: Heavensward launches June 23 on PC, PlayStation 4, and PlayStation 3.
